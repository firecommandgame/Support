Common Questions

Where do I get help for Fire Command?

Email support@firecommandgame.com with your issue, device type, and app version.

How do I report a crash?

Email support with your device model, iOS version, when the crash happened, and any screenshots or recordings available.

Is Fire Command a real emergency service?

No. Fire Command is an emergency response simulation app and is not a substitute for real emergency services. In a real emergency, call your local emergency number.

How long does support take?

Support requests are reviewed as soon as possible. Response times may vary depending on the issue.

Privacy & Safety

Fire Command support will never ask for your password, full payment details, government ID, or sensitive personal information by email. For privacy-related questions, contact support@firecommandgame.com.

Privacy Questions

Email us if you have questions about app privacy, data handling, or account-related concerns.

Emergency Notice

Fire Command is a simulation. For real emergencies, contact emergency services immediately.
