Privacy Policy & Safety

Effective Date: 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the TravelVIP app by Hotel Games. We respect user privacy and only use information as described in this section.

Information We Collect

TravelVIP does not require users to submit sensitive personal information through this support page. If you contact support, we may receive the information you voluntarily provide, such as your name, email address, device type, app version, screenshots, and a description of your issue.

How We Use Information

Information sent to support is used only to respond to support requests, investigate bugs, improve app functionality, and address privacy or safety concerns.

Information Sharing

Hotel Games does not sell personal information. We do not share support request information except when necessary to respond to the request, comply with law, protect users, or operate essential support services.

Children’s Privacy

TravelVIP is not intended to collect personal information from children through this support page. If a parent or guardian believes a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can review and remove it if appropriate.

User Safety

Do not send passwords, payment card numbers, government identification numbers, or other sensitive personal information through the support form or email.
